# 🧠 MindMirror AI

> Understand Your Mind. Improve Your Journey.

MindMirror AI is a Generative AI-powered mental wellness companion designed for students preparing for high-pressure competitive examinations such as **JEE, NEET, UPSC, CAT, GATE, and CUET**.

The platform analyzes daily journal entries, mood logs, study habits, and sleep patterns to identify hidden stress triggers, emotional patterns, burnout risks, and confidence levels while providing personalized coping strategies and motivational support.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Students preparing for competitive examinations often experience:

* Academic stress
* Burnout
* Sleep deprivation
* Self-doubt
* Performance anxiety
* Peer comparison pressure
* Exam-specific challenges

Traditional mood trackers only record emotions.

MindMirror AI goes beyond tracking by uncovering the root causes behind emotional patterns and delivering personalized wellness recommendations.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📝 Daily Wellness Check-In

Students can record:

* Journal Entry
* Mood Score
* Study Hours
* Sleep Hours
* Exam Type

### 🔍 AI Stress Trigger Detection

Identifies common causes of stress such as:

* Mock test anxiety
* Syllabus backlog
* Peer comparison
* Parental expectations
* Time management issues

### 📊 Emotional Pattern Analysis

Analyzes recurring emotional trends and behavioral patterns from journaling history.

### 🔥 Burnout Risk Assessment

Provides intelligent burnout predictions categorized as:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

### 💪 Personalized Coping Strategies

Generates practical recommendations based on the student's emotional state and study behavior.

### 🌟 Motivational Encouragement

Delivers contextual encouragement tailored to the student's exam preparation journey.
