# 🧠 MindMirror AI

> Understand Your Mind. Improve Your Journey.

MindMirror AI is a Generative AI-powered mental wellness companion designed for students preparing for high-pressure competitive examinations such as JEE, NEET, UPSC, CAT, GATE, and CUET.

The platform analyzes daily journal entries, mood logs, study habits, and sleep patterns to identify hidden stress triggers, emotional patterns, burnout risks, and confidence levels while providing personalized coping strategies and motivational support.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Students preparing for competitive examinations often experience:

* Academic stress
* Burnout
* Sleep deprivation
* Self-doubt
* Performance anxiety
* Peer comparison pressure
* Exam-specific challenges

Traditional wellness trackers only record moods.

MindMirror AI goes beyond tracking by uncovering emotional patterns and identifying the root causes behind stress, helping students maintain mental well-being throughout their academic journey.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📝 Daily Wellness Check-In

Students can log:

* Journal Entry
* Mood Score
* Study Hours
* Sleep Hours
* Exam Type

### 🔍 AI Stress Trigger Detection

Identifies stress sources such as:

* Mock test anxiety
* Syllabus backlog
* Peer comparison
* Parental expectations
* Time management challenges

### 📊 Emotional Pattern Analysis

Detects recurring emotional and behavioral trends from daily journaling.

### 🔥 Burnout Risk Assessment

Classifies burnout risk into:

* Low
* Medium
* High

### 💪 Personalized Coping Strategies

Provides tailored recommendations to improve focus, motivation, and emotional balance.

### 🌟 Motivational Encouragement

Generates supportive and contextual encouragement for exam preparation.
