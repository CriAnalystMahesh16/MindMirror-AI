# 🧠 MindMirror AI

> **Understand Your Mind. Improve Your Journey.**

MindMirror AI is an AI-powered mental wellness companion designed specifically for students preparing for high-stakes competitive exams such as **JEE, NEET, UPSC, CAT, GATE, and CUET**.

By analyzing daily journal entries, mood logs, study habits, and sleep patterns, MindMirror AI uncovers hidden stress triggers, emotional patterns, burnout risks, and confidence trends that traditional mood trackers often miss.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Students preparing for competitive examinations face immense pressure from:

* Mock test performance
* Academic competition
* Syllabus backlog
* Fear of failure
* Parental expectations
* Sleep deprivation
* Burnout and emotional fatigue

Traditional wellness trackers only record moods.

MindMirror AI goes beyond tracking and helps students understand **why** they feel stressed and **how** to improve their mental well-being.

---

# 🚀 Solution

MindMirror AI acts as an **AI Emotional Twin**.

Students complete a daily check-in including:

* Journal Entry
* Mood Score
* Study Hours
* Sleep Hours
* Exam Type

The AI analyzes the information and generates:

✅ Stress Trigger Detection

✅ Emotional Pattern Analysis

✅ Burnout Risk Assessment

✅ Confidence Evaluation

✅ Personalized Coping Strategies

✅ Motivational Encouragement

---

# ✨ Key Features

## 📝 Daily Wellness Check-In

Students record:

* Daily reflections
* Mood levels
* Study duration
* Sleep duration

---

## 🔍 Hidden Stress Trigger Detection

The AI identifies recurring causes of stress such as:

* Mock test anxiety
* Peer comparison
* Time pressure
* Family expectations
* Study overload

---

## 📊 Emotional Pattern Recognition

Detects emotional trends across entries.

Example:

> "Your stress levels consistently increase after mock test days."

---

## 🔥 Burnout Risk Analysis

Provides:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

based on behavioral patterns and emotional signals.

---

## 💪 Personalized Coping Strategies

Generates actionable recommendations such as:

* Mindfulness exercises
* Recovery breaks
* Focus improvement tips
* Stress reduction techniques

---

## 🌟 Motivational Support

Provides encouraging messages tailored to the student's current emotional state and progress.
